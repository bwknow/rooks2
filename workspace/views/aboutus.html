The Rooks The Music The Story

The Rooks is made up of four Seattle-area musicians with deep roots in Americana music. Their unique blend of tight vocals, driving rhythm and two-pronged guitar attack makes The Rooks! rock.

The band was originally started by Brian Knowles and Jim Brandt while in junior high school in Seattle Wa., and now have teamed up with Frank James and Bob Virkelyst to continue the tradition.

Like destiny that brought them together again, the band continues to evolve and expand musical boundaries while staying true to Rock roots and creative open spirit.
